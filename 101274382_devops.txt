COMP 3104 fast-tracks you into the IT world, focusing on environments where servers, web apps, and robust infrastructure are key to business success. Gain hands-on experience with proven DevOps technologies like Amazon Web Services, Ansible, Docker, and Travis CI. You'll build your own deployment pipeline, enabling instant web app updates, and witness how effective DevOps strategies can significantly impact a company's bottom line. By the end, you'll master the entire deployment process and understand how DevOps best practices lead to smoother releases, faster fixes, reduced risks, and improved collaboration. This course provides the essential DevOps perspective every developer needs to thrive in today's IT landscape.
