The DevOps course at George Brown College provides students with a comprehensive grasp of the methodologies and tools that integrate software development with IT operations. The program covers essential subjects such as continuous integration, continuous delivery (CI/CD), automation, infrastructure as code, and containerization. Students gain hands-on experience with popular DevOps tools like Git, Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes. By the end of the course, participants will have the skills needed to improve software delivery processes, foster team collaboration, and enhance efficiency in cloud settings. The program equips graduates for roles as DevOps engineers, cloud infrastructure specialists, and systems administrators.
