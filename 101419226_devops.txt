The DevOps course at George Brown College equips students with a thorough understanding of the methodologies and tools that connect software development with IT operations. The curriculum includes key topics such as continuous integration, continuous delivery (CI/CD), automation, infrastructure as code, and containerization. Students acquire practical experience with widely-used DevOps tools like Git, Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes. Upon completing the course, participants will possess the skills necessary to optimize software delivery processes, enhance team collaboration, and boost overall efficiency in cloud environments. The program prepares graduates for careers as DevOps engineers, cloud infrastructure specialists, and systems administrators.
