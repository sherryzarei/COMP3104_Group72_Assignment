The DevOps course at George Brown College provides students with an in-depth understanding of the methodologies and tools used to bridge the gap between software development and IT operations. This course covers essential topics like continuous integration, continuous delivery (CI/CD), automation, infrastructure as code, and containerization. Students gain hands-on experience with popular DevOps tools such as Git, Jenkins, Docker, and Kubernetes. By the end of the course, participants will have the skills to streamline software delivery processes, improve collaboration between teams, and enhance overall efficiency in a cloud-based environment. The program prepares students for roles such as DevOps engineer, cloud infrastructure specialist, and systems administrator.
