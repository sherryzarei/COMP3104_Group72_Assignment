This file is about devops course
The DevOps course at George Brown College provides a comprehensive learning experience, empowering students to master the essential practices and tools for seamless collaboration between development, operations, and security teams. Through a combination of theoretical instruction and practical exercises, students will develop expertise in automation, configuration management, infrastructure as code, and monitoring. Graduates of this program will be well-prepared to streamline software delivery processes, enhance system reliability, and drive innovation within organizations.
