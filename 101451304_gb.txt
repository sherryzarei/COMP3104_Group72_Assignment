George Brown College (GBC), established in 1967, is located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The college is dedicated to providing high-quality education and training to its diverse student body. Under the leadership of Chancellor Noella Milne and President Dr. Gervan Fearon, GBC serves approximately 27,128 full-time students and 58,119 continuing education registrants, offering a variety of programs to meet the needs of the community.
