Established in 1967, George Brown College (GBC) is a prominent institution located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. The college is led by Chancellor Noella Milne and President Dr. Gervan Fearon, who are committed to fostering an inclusive and innovative educational environment. With a student population of 27,128 full-time and 58,119 continuing education registrants, GBC offers a wide range of programs to help students achieve their career goals.
