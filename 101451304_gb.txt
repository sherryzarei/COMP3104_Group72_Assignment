Founded in 1967, George Brown College (GBC) is situated in Toronto, Ontario, Canada, and is known for its vibrant and diverse student community. With Chancellor Noella Milne and President Dr. Gervan Fearon at the helm, GBC supports a total of 27,128 full-time students and 58,119 continuing education registrants, providing a variety of programs designed to cater to the needs of all learners, from traditional degree paths to adult education.
